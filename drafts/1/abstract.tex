\begin{abstract}
This dissertation discusses the composition of \compositionTitle, a notated musical work for ten acoustic instruments.  It will outline the my artistic motivations, theorize about its material foundations in performance practice, explain the system of notation used to create its score, and describe the software developed to aid in its composition and typesetting.  The actual score and full documentation of the software are included in the appendices.
\end{abstract}
